About Us

THE CHOOKIES

The Group's top pick is the "chookies" word, which is a combination of "champurrado" and "cookies." Our company promotes desserts, particularly those with health benefits associated.

The company's goal is to honor proudly made Filipino delicacies bu using sticky rice as the main ingredient.


Chookies is a delicious and healthy dessert, an innovation of the Filipino delicacy Champorado. The product named “Chookies” is derived from the combination of the Champorado which is sweet chocolate rice porridge in the Philippines, and Cookies which is a small sweet cake. The Chookies was made to give a new perspective to the customer, and that is to eat healthy desserts. The Chookies are diet-friendly and contain antioxidants that may protect your cells from free radicals, which may contribute to heart disease, cancer, and other diseases. It has a soft texture because glutinous rice softens the dough. This perfectly sweet cookie comes in a variety of variations, including plain, chocolate chip, and cashew nuts.
